## What It Is

A structured partner ecosystem for organizations helping enterprises adopt Claude. Anthropic built this to scale Claude deployment through consulting firms, ISVs, and technology partners — modeled on the AWS/Azure partner networks but Claude-specific.

## Consulting Partner Requirements

To be listed as a consulting/implementation partner (vs. just a network member):

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| CCA Certification | At least 1 team member must hold CCA credential — hard requirement |
| Production deployments | Minimum 3 documented Claude deployments in production (POCs don't count) |
| Client references | References willing to speak with Anthropic |
| Defined service offering | Specific Claude practice with go-to-market strategy, documented methodology, service tiers |
| Enterprise delivery practices | Data security policies, NDA procedures, professional indemnity insurance, project governance |

**Application process:** 4–8 weeks review + technical interview with Anthropic's partner team
